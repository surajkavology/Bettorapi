Music-based application programming interfaces (APIs) are used by developers to incorporate music and the associated data into a variety of services and applications. A music API's features may include streaming songs or radio stations, displaying lyrics, and providing information about songs, artists, labels, and other metadata. Some music APIs go one step further and offer similar musicians, song recommendations, and even forthcoming live performances by that specific musician or genre.
